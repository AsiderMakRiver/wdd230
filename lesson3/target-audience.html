Target Audience

  1. A description of the target audience
    1. The target of my audience is to find out what weather will be today or on other days. It depends on whether they can change their plans for those days.
  2. Two personas
    1. Joshua is 23 years old. Married. He likes cooking and drive to nature and look at the mountains with his wife. He is studying at the University and using a smartphone well but does not use social media often.
    2. John is 25 years old. Single. He likes playing video games and watch anime. He has a temporary job and studying on PC and using a smartphone well. He likes to walk with his friends on street and to talk.
  3. At least five listed scenarios
    1. Most visitors looking if the rain will be.
    2. Looking to know what clothes to dress in today.
    3. Looking to find out whether to prepare outdoor activities or not.
    4. Looking to build their plan for the weekend.
    5. Looking to build the infostructure transport.
