Cinnamon Rolls

By Alane Watkins, produced by Clawsons

Ingredients || Ингредиенты

  • Warm water: 2 1/2 cups || 550ml
  • Sugar: 1/2cup || 110gr
  • Yeast: 4Tbsp || 45gr
  • Eggs: 2
  • Salt: 1Tbsp || 20gr
  • Oil: 1/2 cup || 118ml
  • Flour: 7-8 cups || 1100-1209gr
  • Cinnamon: Over the entire surface || 15-20gr
  • Brown sugar: Over the entire surface ||
  • Dental floss(optional)

Frosting || Глазурь

  • Sour cream: 8 ouncez || 227gr
  • Soft Butter: 2 sticks || 227gr
  • Vanilla sugar: 1Tbsp || 1 packet
  • Powder sugar 4 cups || 436gr

Directions

Step 1 Make a dough

Take a bowl. Combine warm water, sugar and yeast. Mix everythink togather. Use a spoon or something. Let rise for 10 minutes. Add two eggsa, salt and oil. Mix everythink togather. Add a little bit of flour at a time. After let the dough rest and double in size or put it in a warm place for about 30 min.

Step 2 Make a rolls

After when the dough will grou up. Separate the dough in half. Prepare the clean surface and put a flour on the surface, after add the dough. Roll dough into a rectangle. The dough should be the middle thick
Right know you can preheat your oven to 350F or 176C. No continuem. Melt a little bit of butter and grease the top of dough and put on the top sinamon and brown shugar over the entire surface. After Roll up the dough.
Right now I will recomend to use dental floss to cut the cinnamons rolls because the place of cutting will more pretier than if you wore use the nife for esample. After pices of rolls put on the pallet amd let cinnamon rolls double in size(for 30 min). After bake it for 18-20 minets and check them early.

Step 3 Frosting

Take a bowl and add 8 onces of sour cream and 2 stick of soft butter. Mix all of that togather. After add 1Tbsp of vannila sugar and add 4 cups of powdered sugar but the last add with little pices. Mixed.

Step 4 Сompletion

After when your cinnamons rolls is baked wait for a 15 minutes and add a frosting on the top.
